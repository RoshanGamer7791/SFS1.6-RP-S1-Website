Whats Prestige?

Prestige is a cosmetic score to measure how much novel things your program has done.

You get 2 prestige for first manned events.

You get 1 prestige for first unmanned events.

You get 0.25 prestige for first uncontrollable unmanned events (though if a unmanned probe already did what your uncontrollable craft reached then it dosen't count)

Claims

If you land on a celestial body after completing the required progression (orbit → flyby → landing), you may claim up to 50% ownership of that body. For captured asteroids, Phobos, and Deimos, a direct landing is sufficient. Claim percentage scales with base size and permanence. Trading of territorial claims is allowed. If an agency controls more than 80% of a moon or planet (excluding Phobos, Deimos, and captured asteroids), they may temporarily launch from that body by changing the Space Center settings file. After the launch, the Space Center must be reverted back to Earth. You must also rebuild infrastructure so at first you only have suborbital tech on that, etc.
